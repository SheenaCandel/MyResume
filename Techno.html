Project Description

This project is a compliance for our Technopreneurship subject. It's about building a business that's related to our program. GOcery is a Grocery Ordering System that has partnered the local stores in Cagayan de Oro City. It's a proposed solution during the pandemic when COVID19 has spread around the city and precautions has a lot more strict. People could order in the comfort of their home.
